What is madhavseva ?

Madhav Seva is an effort to provide an opportunity to devotees to offer heir loving service to Shri Shri Radha Venimadhav and thus establish a connection with their Lordships. Everyday Their Lordships are offered many services aratis, bhogas and Shringaars as per the age old traditions of Gaudiya Vaishnavism. Through Madhav Seva, we're sharing this opportunity with one and all.
